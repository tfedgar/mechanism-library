Phosphodiesterase-5

PDE5A

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

PDE5 (PDE5A) breaks down cGMP in vascular smooth muscle. Inhibitors (tadalafil/Cialis, sildenafil/Viagra) raise cGMP → nitric-oxide-mediated vasodilation → improved erections, and lowered blood pressure. ESTABLISHED: treats the SYMPTOM of erectile dysfunction; does not fix the underlying cause (which in PED users may be high/low estradiol, high prolactin, raised blood pressure, endothelial dysfunction, or post-cycle low testosterone). Reported issues linked: ED management; also used by some for blood-pressure/pump effects. Addressing root hormonal causes is the mechanistic fix.
