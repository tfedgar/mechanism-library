Reported issue: raised blood pressure. CANDIDATE MECHANISMS (competing, additive): (1) Sodium/water retention (mineralocorticoid MR/ENaC + estradiol). (2) Raised hematocrit/viscosity (erythropoiesis). (3) Endothelial/NO dysfunction (NOS3) and arterial stiffness. (4) Stimulants/other drugs; sleep apnea (worsened by mass/TRT). Falsifiable check: hematocrit, sodium/fluid status, response to ARB vs phlebotomy vs dose reduction; ambulatory BP.
