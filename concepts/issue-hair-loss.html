Reported issue: accelerated scalp hair loss. CANDIDATE MECHANISM (ESTABLISHED, in genetically susceptible men): androgens → 5-alpha-reductase (SRD5A) → DHT → AR in dermal papilla → follicle miniaturization. More androgenic / DHT-derivative compounds accelerate it. 5-ARi (finasteride/dutasteride) reduce DHT but have their own side-effect debate (and blunt some androgenic effect). Falsifiable check: pattern (androgenic vs telogen effluvium), response to 5-ARi/topicals; family history.
