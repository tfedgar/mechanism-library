melanin-concentrating hormone receptor 1

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Melanin-concentrating hormone receptor 1 (MCHR1) is a G protein-coupled receptor that mediates the effects of melanin-concentrating hormone, an orexigenic peptide regulating energy homeostasis. Activation of this receptor stimulates the hypothalamic-pituitary axis, leading to the secretion of growth hormone and influencing body weight and composition PMID 16603725. This mechanism distinguishes it from the melanocortin-1 receptor, which primarily drives pigmentation and photoprotection via the cAMP pathway PMID 24807163.
