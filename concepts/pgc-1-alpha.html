PGC-1 alpha

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

PGC-1α is a master transcriptional coactivator that regulates cellular energy metabolism and mitochondrial biogenesis in high-energy tissues like muscle and heart [Established] 42319436). It functions by interacting with diverse transcription factors to orchestrate adaptive responses to metabolic stress and cold exposure [Evidence] 30809287). This coordination is critical for maintaining energy homeostasis, as dysfunction in PGC-1α signaling is linked to neurodegeneration and metabolic syndromes [Evidence] 41268687).
