beta-catenin

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Beta-catenin is a key protein in the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway that regulates stem cell proliferation and differentiation [Established] PMID 40448062. It acts as a central mediator in bone homeostasis, where its levels increase in osteoblasts to promote proliferation and regulate bone remodeling [Evidence] PMID 25866509. Dysregulation of this pathway influences cell fate determination, linking it to conditions such as cancer and osteoarthritis [Established] PMID 34980884.
