Amylin is a neuroendocrine polypeptide hormone co-secreted with insulin by pancreatic β-cells that regulates energy homeostasis and appetite Established 38338796). It lowers postprandial glucose by delaying gastric emptying and suppressing glucagon, while promoting satiety through central nervous system pathways Established 41549439).
