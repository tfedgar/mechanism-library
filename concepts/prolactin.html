prolactin

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Prolactin is a pituitary hormone that regulates lactation and influences over 300 physiological processes, including metabolism and immune regulation [Established] [ 33703987]. Mechanistically, it impairs erectile function by suppressing endothelial nitric oxide synthase (eNOS) independently of testosterone levels [Evidence] [ 40848920]. This direct vascular effect explains why men with prolactinomas often experience erectile dysfunction even when testosterone remains normal [Evidence] [ 40848920].
