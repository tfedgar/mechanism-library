ISSUE: Polycythemia / high hematocrit

issue-map

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Reported issue: rising hematocrit/hemoglobin on androgens. CANDIDATE MECHANISM (largely single, ESTABLISHED): AR-driven increase in erythropoietin (EPO/EPOR) plus SUPPRESSION of hepcidin (HAMP)/raised erythroferrone (ERFE) → more iron for erythropoiesis → raised red cell mass. Worse with testosterone, boldenone, oxymetholone. Downstream: viscosity → hypertension and thrombosis risk. Falsifiable check: hematocrit/EPO/ferritin trend; response to dose reduction or phlebotomy.
