Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Reported issue: infertility, shrunken testes. CANDIDATE MECHANISM (ESTABLISHED, INDIRECT): exogenous androgens suppress GnRH → LH/FSH → collapse of intratesticular testosterone and spermatogenesis (HPG shutdown). This is NOT a primary-target toxicity; it is feedback. Often reversible with time, hCG (LHCGR), and SERMs (clomiphene → LH/FSH), though long/high-dose use can delay or limit recovery. Falsifiable check: LH/FSH (suppressed), semen analysis, response to hCG/SERM restart.
