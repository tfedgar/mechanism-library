MECHANISMS

Mineralocorticoid / fluid

NR3C2 (MR) / ENaC

Some AAS and their estrogenic effects promote sodium and water retention via mineralocorticoid-receptor (NR3C2/MR) -> ENaC sodium channel activity in the kidney, and via raised estradiol. ESTABLISHED: fluid retention raises blood pressure and causes the 'bloated/smooth' look. Spironolactone antagonizes MR (and is also anti-androgenic). Reported issues linked: water retention, edema, raised blood pressure; the dangerous post-show scenario where competitors stop diuretics abruptly and rebound-retain fluid.
