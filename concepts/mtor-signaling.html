mTOR signaling

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

mTOR signaling is a cellular pathway that coordinates activities like metabolism and reproduction in response to environmental cues [Established] 41924822). It drives anabolic processes such as protein synthesis and skeletal muscle hypertrophy by maintaining a balance with catabolic signals like AMPK [Evidence] 41356921). Mechanically, mTORC1 regulates ultrastructural adaptations like myofibrillogenesis during mechanical loading [Evidence] 42239245).
