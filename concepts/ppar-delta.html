PPAR-delta

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

PPAR-delta is a ubiquitously expressed nuclear receptor that acts as a ligand-activated transcription factor to regulate fatty acid oxidation, glucose metabolism, and inflammation [Established] PMID 30373124 PMID 39899669. It maintains metabolic homeostasis by controlling genes involved in lipid and bile acid processing, particularly within the liver [Established] PMID 39899669. Mechanistically, it influences disease states through both induction and transrepression of target genes, exhibiting complex roles in neuroinflammation and cancer progression [Evidence] PMID 33210212 PMID 33808880.
