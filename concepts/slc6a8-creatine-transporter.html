SLC6A8 creatine transporter

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

The SLC6A8 creatine transporter is a protein that moves creatine into cells, such as skeletal muscle and CD8+ T-cells, to support energy metabolism [Established] PMID 42255854. By facilitating the uptake of creatine, it enables the formation of a phosphocreatine pool that buffers ATP levels and aids rapid energy resynthesis during high demand [Established] PMID 39796530. This mechanism is critical for maintaining cellular energy status and has been linked to enhanced immune function and spermatogenesis [Evidence] PMID 42255854, PMID 39730393.
