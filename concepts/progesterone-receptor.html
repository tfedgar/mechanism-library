Progesterone receptor

PGR

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

The progesterone receptor (PGR) is activated by progestogenic steroids. ESTABLISHED: 19-nortestosterone derivatives (nandrolone, trenbolone) have intrinsic PROGESTOGENIC activity at PGR, which (alongside raised prolactin) drives gynecomastia and sexual side effects INDEPENDENT of estradiol. This explains why E2 control alone (an AI) often fails to prevent 'nandrolone/tren gyno' — the progestin/prolactin route must be addressed. Reported issues linked: gynecomastia and libido/erectile problems on 19-nor compounds resistant to AIs.
