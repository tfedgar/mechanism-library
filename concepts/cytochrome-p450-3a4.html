Cytochrome P450 3A4 (CYP3A4) is a heme-containing oxidoreductase enzyme that catalyzes the oxidation of a wide variety of xenobiotics, including drugs and environmental compounds [ 39682707]. It plays a critical role in drug metabolism by converting substrates into different products through bioactivation or detoxification reactions [ 30717606]. This metabolic activity determines the clearance rates and potential interactions of many commonly prescribed medications. [Established]
