inositol trisphosphate

Inositol trisphosphate is a lipid-derived second messenger that orchestrates calcium dynamics and signal transduction by releasing intracellular calcium stores PMID 41032702. It matters mechanistically because it acts as a primary mediator in numerous cellular processes, including membrane trafficking and metabolic homeostasis. This role is part of the broader phosphoinositide signaling cascade that regulates spatial and temporal cellular responses. [Established]
