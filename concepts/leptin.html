leptin

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Leptin is an adipokine produced by fat cells that signals the brain about total energy stores to regulate food intake and energy balance [Established] 26903647). It acts on multiple brain circuits to control autonomic outflow and endocrine function, thereby maintaining metabolic homeostasis [Established] 26903647). In obesity, leptin resistance diminishes this signaling, which disrupts both metabolic control and hippocampal synaptic plasticity [Evidence] 39594988).
