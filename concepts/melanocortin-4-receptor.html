melanocortin 4 receptor

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

The melanocortin 4 receptor (MC4R) is a G protein-coupled receptor that plays a central role in energy homeostasis by suppressing appetite and increasing energy expenditure Established [ 34433901]. It functions within the leptin-melanocortin pathway, where activation by peptides like α-MSH leads to reduced food intake and weight regulation Established [ 39713072]. Beyond metabolism, MC4R is also implicated in respiratory control, particularly in obesity-related sleep-disordered breathing Evidence [ 41955284].
