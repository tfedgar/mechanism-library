11beta-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 2

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

11beta-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase 2 [Established] is an enzyme that inactivates glucocorticoids, thereby protecting tissues from excessive cortisol exposure PMID 22273746. This mechanism is critical because the enzyme prevents high levels of active steroid hormones from binding to and overstimulating mineralocorticoid receptors PMID 22273746. By regulating local hormone levels, it helps maintain metabolic and cardiovascular homeostasis PMID 22273746.
