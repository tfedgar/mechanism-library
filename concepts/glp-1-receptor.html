GLP-1 receptor

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

The GLP-1 receptor is a protein target that, when activated, regulates appetite and caloric intake by influencing neurons in brain regions controlling satiety [Established]. This activation suppresses hunger and modulates energy expenditure through central and peripheral pathways [ 39892489]. It also stimulates insulin secretion in pancreatic β-cells while inhibiting glucagon release, thereby improving glucose control [ 39114288].
