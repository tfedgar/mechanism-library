MECHANISMS

GLP-1 / GIP receptors

GLP1R / GIPR

GLP-1 receptor agonists (semaglutide) and dual GLP-1/GIP agonists (tirzepatide) activate incretin receptors (GLP1R, GIPR) -> glucose-dependent insulin secretion, suppressed glucagon, SLOWED GASTRIC EMPTYING, and central appetite suppression -> weight loss and improved glycemia. ESTABLISHED side effects: nausea/vomiting/GI upset (gastric emptying), and concern over loss of lean mass with rapid weight loss; gallbladder events; rare pancreatitis signal. Reported issues linked: nausea/GI, 'Ozempic' appetite loss, muscle-loss worry, injection-site and dosing issues. Distinct from steroids — a metabolic, not androgen, pathway.
