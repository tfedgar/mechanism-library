MECHANISMS

ISSUE: Gynecomastia

issue-map

Reported issue: gynecomastia (breast tissue) on androgens. CANDIDATE MECHANISMS (competing): (1) ESTROGENIC — aromatizable AAS -> CYP19A1 -> estradiol -> ESR1 in breast (treat with AI or SERM); raised free-E2 if SHBG is low on orals. (2) PROGESTIN/PROLACTIN — 19-nortestosterones (nandrolone, trenbolone) act at PGR and raise prolactin (PRLR), driving gyno that an AI alone does NOT fix (needs a dopamine agonist / SERM). Distinguish by compound: pure aromatizing (test) vs 19-nor. Falsifiable check: estradiol and prolactin levels; response to AI vs cabergoline.
