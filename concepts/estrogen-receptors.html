MECHANISMS

Estrogen receptors

ESR1 / ESR2

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Estrogen receptors alpha (ESR1) and beta (ESR2) mediate estradiol's effects. SERMs (selective ER modulators) are tissue-selective: tamoxifen and raloxifene ANTAGONIZE ER in breast tissue (treat/prevent gynecomastia) while acting as agonists elsewhere (bone, lipids). Clomiphene/enclomiphene ANTAGONIZE hypothalamic ER, removing estrogen negative feedback -> increased GnRH -> LH/FSH -> endogenous testosterone (used for restart/PCT and as a TRT alternative). Reported issues linked: gynecomastia (ER in breast), and for SERM users: visual disturbance (clomiphene), mood. SERMs treat the RECEPTOR (gyno) whereas AIs cut estrogen SYNTHESIS — different points in the pathway.
