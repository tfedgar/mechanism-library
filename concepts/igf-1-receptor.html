IGF-1 receptor

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

The IGF-1 receptor (IGF1R) is a cell-surface protein that transduces signals from insulin-like growth factor 1 to regulate growth and metabolism [Established]. Mechanistically, it activates downstream pathways like PI3K/Akt/mTOR to boost protein synthesis and inhibit degradation, driving skeletal muscle hypertrophy [Evidence] 32858949). It also supports embryonic development and modulates neuronal excitability to influence the reproductive axis [Evidence] 41799434, 41423875).
