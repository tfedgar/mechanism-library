Sex hormone-binding globulin

SHBG

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

SHBG is a liver-made carrier that binds testosterone and estradiol in blood; only FREE (unbound) hormone is active. ESTABLISHED: oral 17-alpha-alkylated AAS strongly LOWER SHBG, raising free fractions of both testosterone and estradiol even when total levels look modest — so a 'normal' total testosterone can hide a high free level, and estrogenic effects can appear unexpectedly. Thyroid status, insulin/insulin resistance, and obesity also move SHBG. Reported issues linked: misleading bloodwork interpretation; unexpected estrogenic effects on orals; libido changes.
