Reported issue: crashed HDL / raised LDL. CANDIDATE MECHANISM (ESTABLISHED): oral 17aa AAS induce HEPATIC LIPASE (LIPC) and shift lipid handling → sharply lower HDL, higher LDL/ApoB. Falsifiable check: lipid panel on vs off the oral; partial response to statin/ezetimibe but HDL recovers mainly on cessation.
