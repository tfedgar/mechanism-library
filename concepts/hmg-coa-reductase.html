HMG-CoA reductase

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

HMG-CoA reductase is the enzyme that catalyzes the rate-limiting step of converting HMG-CoA to mevalonate in cholesterol biosynthesis [ 22433938]. It matters mechanistically because its inhibition by statins reduces cholesterol synthesis and is also regulated by AMPK to modulate energy homeostasis. This enzyme serves as the primary target for commonly prescribed medications used to manage elevated lipid levels and cardiovascular disease.
