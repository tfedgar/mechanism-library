MECHANISMS

Prolactin & dopamine

PRLR / DRD2

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Prolactin acts via its receptor (PRLR); dopamine tonically SUPPRESSES prolactin via pituitary D2 receptors (DRD2). ESTABLISHED: 19-nortestosterone steroids (nandrolone/deca, trenbolone) raise prolactin (partly via progestogenic activity) → high prolactin causes gynecomastia, low libido, and erectile dysfunction ('deca/tren dick'). Dopamine agonists (cabergoline, pramipexole) lower prolactin via DRD2 → used to manage this. KEY: aromatase inhibitors do NOT fix prolactin/progestin-driven gyno from 19-nors — the mechanism is different. Reported issues linked: gynecomastia and ED specifically on nandrolone/trenbolone; lactation; low libido.
