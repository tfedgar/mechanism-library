Aromatase

CYP19A1

Mechanism at a glance

molecularcellulareffectcaveat

Aromatase (CYP19A1) converts testosterone → estradiol (E2) and androstenedione → estrone in fat, gonad, brain, bone. More aromatizable substrate (high-dose testosterone) → more E2. ESTABLISHED E2 actions in men: breast tissue proliferation (gynecomastia, with prolactin/progestin as co-factors), favorable lipids, bone density, libido, mood, water/sodium balance, and feedback on LH. Aromatase inhibitors (anastrozole, exemestane, letrozole) block this. KEY CAVEAT: E2 has a U-shaped risk curve — both excess AND crashed E2 are harmful (low E2 → joint pain, low libido, worse lipids, bone loss, mood). 'More AI = safer' is WRONG. Reported issues linked: gynecomastia, water retention/blood pressure, moodiness, libido changes, joint pain (often from over-suppression).
